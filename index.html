Previous Projects

  • PhD Project: Creating Deep Models of Human Genome Replication
    • Processed and analysed genome-wide genomics datasets from a wide range of human cell types.
    • Generated insights into the relationship between DNA sequence and replication timing (RT).
    • Developed and scaled PyTorch deep learning models of sequence-driven RT on long DNA sequence (100Kbp).
    • Implemented model augmentations that enforced reverse-complement equivariance in model architecture
    • Extended the modelling to cell-type specific RT predictions with ATAC-seq data.
  • OxRAM Robot Arm: Building and Programming a 6 DOF Robot Arm to play Chess
  • Master's Project: Machine Learning Models of EEG Activity in Remittent Depression
    • Analysed transient stable states of human brain electric field from depressed/control patient EEG data.
    • Identified and extracted key super-states (microstates) in MATLAB (EEGLAB).
    • Optimised a machine learning model in Python to predict remission from dynamics of EEG microstates.
